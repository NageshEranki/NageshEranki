Project Goal

This project was concerned with exploring the relationship between position feedback data-rates and a quadrotors' station-keeping performance. To this end, I performed system identification (mass, moments of inertia, thrust profile), control synthesis, model-in-loop, software-in-loop and finally, flights tests on a physical quadrotor.
